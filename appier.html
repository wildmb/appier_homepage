Appier

Our Vision

Founded with the mission to make global mobile marketing easier and happier, Appier utilizes the power of Artificial Intelligence and data mining to make every single advertising impression smart and cost-effective, and aspires to help our customers achieve their business goals and maximize their ROI in the global market.

Our International Team

Appier is formed by a group of passionate computer scientists, data analysts, and online marketers with substantial experience in AI, data mining and digital marketing. Our multicultural team members come from Intel, Yahoo, Group M, renowned game companies, as well as world class AI and data mining research groups from Stanford and Harvard University.
